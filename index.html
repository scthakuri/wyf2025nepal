Welcome to WYF 2025 Nepal (“we”, “our”, or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application WYF 2025 Nepal (the “App”). By using the App you agree to the terms below. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Country and organization (if applicable)
  • Profile photo (optional)

b. Event and Usage Information

  • Session attendance or check-ins
  • App usage data (pages/features used)
  • Preferences for sessions, speakers, and events

c. Device Information

  • Device type, model and OS version
  • IP address and device identifiers
  • App version and crash/error logs (for improving performance)
2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Register and manage your festival account
  • Provide event schedules, updates and announcements
  • Facilitate participant networking (if enabled)
  • Improve and personalize the app experience
  • Respond to support requests and technical issues
  • Ensure app functionality, security, and reliability
3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share limited data when necessary:

  • With event organizers and partners to manage festival activities
  • With service providers (hosting, analytics, communications) who help operate the App
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
4. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to support event-related activities and legal obligations. After the festival ends, most personal data will be deleted or anonymized within 90 days, unless you opt to remain connected for future WYF events.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your stored data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: wyf2025nepal@gmail.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for participants aged 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will promptly remove it.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may include links or integrations with third-party services (e.g., Google Maps, social media). These services have their own privacy practices. We are not responsible for third-party policies or actions—please review their privacy policies before use.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App and on our website with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
